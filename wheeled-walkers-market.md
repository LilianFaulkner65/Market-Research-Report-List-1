<p><p>Wheeled walkers are classified into three main types: two-wheeled, three-wheeled, and four-wheeled. Two-wheeled walkers offer minimal support and are typically lightweight, ideal for individuals with moderate stability. Three-wheeled walkers provide enhanced maneuverability, making them suitable for indoor and outdoor use, accommodating those who require more support while being agile. Four-wheeled walkers, or rollators, feature a stable base with hand brakes and a seat, offering maximum support and convenience for users needing extra stability during longer walks or rest stops.</p></p>
