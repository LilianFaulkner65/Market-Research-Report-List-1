<p><ul><li>Centrifugation</li><li>Membrane Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The apheresis equipment market consists of two primary types: centrifugation and membrane filtration. Centrifugation utilizes centrifugal force to separate blood components based on density, effectively isolating target elements such as platelets or plasma. In contrast, membrane filtration employs selectively permeable membranes to separate blood components based on size and solubility, allowing for the extraction of specific substances. Both methods are essential in therapeutic procedures, blood donation, and research applications, contributing significantly to advancements in medical treatments and patient care.</p></p>
