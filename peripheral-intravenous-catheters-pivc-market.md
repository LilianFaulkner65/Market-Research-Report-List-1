<p><p>The Peripheral Intravenous Catheter (PIVC) market encompasses two main types: ported and non-ported catheters. Ported PIVCs feature an access port, allowing for easier medication delivery and sample collection without repeated needle insertion, enhancing patient comfort and reducing infection risk. Non-ported PIVCs, however, lack this access point and are primarily used for short-term intravenous therapy. Both types are essential in clinical settings, catering to various patient needs and treatment durations while impacting overall healthcare efficiency and safety.</p></p>
