<p><p>Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) is an advanced technology that uses optical fibers to measure temperature along their entire length, providing real-time and continuous temperature monitoring. The DTS market is categorized into Multi-Mode DTS and Single-Mode DTS. Multi-Mode DTS utilizes multiple light paths within optical fibers, allowing for shorter sensing distances with higher accuracy, ideal for specific industrial applications. In contrast, Single-Mode DTS employs a single light path, offering longer sensing ranges and improved sensitivity, making it suitable for extensive monitoring applications like pipelines and power cables.</p></p>
