<p><ul><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalysts (DOC)</li><li>Catalyzed Soot Filters (CSF)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li><li>Three-Way Conversion Catalyst (TWC)</li><li>Motorcycle Catalysts</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile emission catalyst market encompasses various types designed to reduce harmful emissions from vehicles. Diesel Oxidation Catalysts (DOC) primarily convert carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons from diesel exhaust. Catalyzed Soot Filters (CSF) trap and oxidize particulate matter. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) utilizes a urea solution to transform nitrogen oxides into harmless gases. Three-Way Conversion Catalysts (TWC) balance the emissions from gasoline engines. Motorcycle Catalysts address two-wheeler emissions, while "Others" includes niche catalytic solutions, reflecting diverse emission control technologies.</p></p>
