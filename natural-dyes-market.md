<p><p>The natural dyes market comprises plant-based, animal-based, and mineral-based dyes, each with unique sources and applications. Mineral-based dyes, like ocher, are sourced from earth pigments, yielding earthy tones like yellows and browns. These dyes are valued for their eco-friendliness, sustainability, and historical significance in textiles, art, and cosmetics.</p></p>
