<p><p>The Personal Care UV Filter market comprises two main types: organic and inorganic UV filters. Organic UV filters are chemical compounds that absorb UV radiation, often providing a lightweight feel and good cosmetic compatibility. They are commonly used in formulations for sunscreens and skin care products. In contrast, inorganic UV filters, such as zinc oxide and titanium dioxide, physically block UV rays and are favored for their broad-spectrum protection and skin-friendly properties, making them popular in mineral-based cosmetics.</p></p>
