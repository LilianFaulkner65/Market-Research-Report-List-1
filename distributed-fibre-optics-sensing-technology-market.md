<p><strong>The Distributed Fibre Optics Sensing Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OTDR</li><li>OFDR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Fibre Optics Sensing Technology encompasses various methods for monitoring physical parameters over long distances using optical fibres. Two prominent market types include Optical Time Domain Reflectometry (OTDR) and Optical Frequency Domain Reflectometry (OFDR). OTDR is primarily used for fault detection and event location along fibre networks, providing quick assessments. In contrast, OFDR offers high-resolution measurements of strain and temperature, making it ideal for applications in civil engineering and environmental monitoring, allowing for continuous and accurate data collection.</p></p>
