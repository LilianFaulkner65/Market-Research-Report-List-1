<p><p>The automotive EGR system market comprises various components essential for reducing emissions. EGR valves control the flow of exhaust gases back into the intake manifold. EGR coolers cool these gases to improve efficiency and reduce the potential for knocking. EGR sensors monitor the flow and temperature of the gases to ensure optimal performance and compliance with regulations. The ECU (Engine Control Unit) manages the entire EGR process, adjusting parameters to enhance fuel efficiency and minimize harmful emissions.</p></p>
