<p><p>The Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) program consists of three levels, each focusing on different aspects of finance. Level I emphasizes investment tools, covering foundational topics like ethics, quantitative methods, and financial reporting. Level II dives into asset valuation, teaching advanced analysis of securities, portfolio valuation, and financial instruments. Level III shifts to portfolio management, focusing on applying concepts to manage investment portfolios, incorporating strategies, risk management, and client interactions for effective investment decision-making. Together, these levels create a comprehensive framework for financial expertise.</p></p>
