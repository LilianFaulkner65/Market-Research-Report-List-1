<p><p>The TWS (True Wireless Stereo) in-ear monitor market comprises two primary types of noise cancellation technologies: active noise cancellation (ANC) and passive noise cancellation. ANC uses electronic processing to detect external sounds and produce counteracting sound waves, effectively reducing ambient noise. In contrast, passive noise canceling relies on physical design and materials to block out external sound by creating a seal in the ear canal. Both types enhance audio experience, but they cater to different user needs and environments.</p></p>
