<p><strong>The Ignition Control Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Sensor</li><li>Hall Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ignition control module market is primarily segmented into two main types based on sensor technology: inductive sensors and Hall sensors. Inductive sensors operate on the principle of magnetic induction to detect the position of the crankshaft and camshaft, making them reliable for traditional ignition systems. Conversely, Hall sensors utilize magnetic fields to determine rotational position, offering enhanced accuracy and responsiveness, especially in modern vehicles. Both types play crucial roles in engine timing and efficiency, reflecting advancements in automotive technology.</p></p>
