<p><p>The Automatic Tourniquet System market comprises two primary types: pneumatic tourniquets and electric tourniquets. Pneumatic tourniquets utilize air pressure to occlude blood flow, offering precise control and adaptability during surgical procedures. They are often favored for their reliability and ease of use. Electric tourniquets, on the other hand, employ electronic mechanisms to maintain pressure, providing enhanced automation and monitoring capabilities. Both types are crucial in medical settings, enhancing patient safety and surgical efficiency by minimizing blood loss.</p></p>
