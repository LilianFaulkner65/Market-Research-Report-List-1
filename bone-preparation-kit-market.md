<p><p>The Bone Preparation Kit market is categorized into two main types: needle and needleless systems. Needle-based kits involve traditional methods using hollow needles to aspirate or inject materials into bone, often providing precise control. In contrast, needleless kits utilize advanced technologies, such as high-pressure mechanisms or other non-invasive techniques, to prepare bone tissue without the use of needles, enhancing patient comfort and safety. Each type caters to varying procedural needs and preferences in orthopedic surgery and other medical applications.</p></p>
