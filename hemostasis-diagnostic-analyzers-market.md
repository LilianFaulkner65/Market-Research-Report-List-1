<p><p>Hemostasis diagnostic analyzers are crucial for assessing coagulation processes in patients. The market comprises two primary types: open test tube systems and enclosed injection systems. Open test tube systems allow manual sample handling, offering flexibility but requiring more technician involvement. Each type serves specific clinical needs, with open systems typically used in research settings and enclosed systems favored in clinical laboratories for their efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
