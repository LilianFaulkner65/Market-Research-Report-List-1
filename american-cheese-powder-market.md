<p><p>The American cheese powder market is primarily divided into two types: natural cheese powder and processed cheese powder. Natural cheese powder is derived from real cheese through a drying process, retaining flavor and nutritional value, making it popular in gourmet applications. In contrast, processed cheese powder combines cheese with emulsifiers and additives, offering a consistent flavor and better solubility, making it ideal for snacks and convenience foods. Both types cater to diverse culinary uses, from seasonings to ready-to-eat meals.</p></p>
