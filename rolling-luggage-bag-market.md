They offer stability but may require some effort to pull over uneven terrain. </p><p>In contrast, 4-wheel luggage features four multi-directional wheels, allowing for effortless movement in any direction. This design enhances mobility, making it easier to navigate crowded areas and tight spaces, appealing to travelers seeking convenience.</p></p>
