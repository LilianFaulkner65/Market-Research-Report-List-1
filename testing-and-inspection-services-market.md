<p><p>The Testing and Inspection Services market encompasses various types aimed at ensuring product quality and compliance throughout the production process. Initial Production Check (IPC) evaluates raw materials and initial production samples. During Production Check (DUPRO) assesses quality while manufacturing is ongoing. Final Random Inspection (FRI) provides a comprehensive evaluation of finished goods before shipment. Loading/Unloading Supervision (LS/US) ensures proper handling during transport. Other services may include factory audits and product testing, catering to diverse industry needs for quality assurance.</p></p>
