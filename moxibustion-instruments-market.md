<p><p>Moxibustion instruments are categorized into two main types: open flame and no open flame. Open flame moxibustion utilizes direct heat from ignited moxa sticks or cones applied to specific acupuncture points, promoting warmth and circulation. This traditional method is revered for its therapeutic effects but requires careful handling. No open flame moxibustion, on the other hand, employs indirect methods such as electronic devices or pre-prepared moxa products that generate heat without direct combustion, enhancing safety and user-friendliness.</p></p>
