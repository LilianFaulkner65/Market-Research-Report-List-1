<p><p>Centrifugal sieves are categorized into two main market types: single centrifugal chamber and multiple centrifugal chamber. Single centrifugal chamber sieves utilize one chamber to separate particles based on size and density, offering simplicity and efficiency for smaller operations. In contrast, multiple centrifugal chamber sieves feature several chambers, allowing for simultaneous processing of larger volumes and enhanced separation capabilities. This versatility makes them suitable for higher production needs, addressing various industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
