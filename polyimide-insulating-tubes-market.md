<p><ul><li>Seamless Polyimide Tube</li><li>Spiral Wound Polyimide Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyimide Insulating Tubes market consists of two main types: Seamless Polyimide Tubes and Spiral Wound Polyimide Tubes. Seamless Polyimide Tubes are manufactured without joints, offering superior structural integrity and consistent insulation properties, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, Spiral Wound Polyimide Tubes are created by winding polyimide film into a cylindrical shape, providing flexibility and ease of installation. Both types are valued for their excellent thermal stability and electrical insulation capabilities in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
