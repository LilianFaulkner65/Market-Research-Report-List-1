<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multimedia projectors come in various types, primarily including DLP (Digital Light Processing), LCD (Liquid Crystal Display), and others such as LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors utilize a digital micromirror device for dynamic imagery, offering high contrast and movement fluidity, ideal for home theaters. LCD projectors use panels to produce bright images with accurate colors, suitable for presentations. Other types like LCoS combine LCD and DLP technologies for enhanced image quality, catering to specific needs across education, business, and entertainment sectors.</p></p>
