<p><p>Asphalt shingles roofing materials primarily come in two types: organic and fiberglass. Organic asphalt shingles are made with a cellulose fiber base, offering excellent durability and moisture resistance but can be heavier and less environmentally friendly. Fiberglass asphalt shingles utilize a fiberglass mat for reinforcement, making them lighter and more fire-resistant while also providing better dimensional stability. Both types are popular for their affordability, versatility, and aesthetic appeal, catering to a wide range of roofing applications and styles.</p></p>
