<p><p>The AMR Sensing ICs market is categorized into two main types: Digital Output AMR Sensing ICs and Analog Output AMR Sensing ICs. Digital Output AMR Sensing ICs convert magnetic fields into digital signals, offering precise measurements with simple interfacing capabilities for digital systems. In contrast, Analog Output AMR Sensing ICs produce continuous voltage signals proportional to the magnetic field strength, enabling detailed waveform analysis. Each type serves distinct applications based on requirements for signal processing and system integration.</p></p>
