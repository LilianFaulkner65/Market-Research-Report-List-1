<p><p>The glass-lined evaporator market can be segmented into two main types: L-Rotor and P-Rotor. L-Rotor evaporators feature a long, cylindrical shape that enhances heat transfer efficiency and is ideal for handling viscous materials. P-Rotor evaporators, on the other hand, have a more compact, paddle-like design, allowing for effective mixing and evaporation of lower-viscosity liquids. Both types are widely used in chemical, pharmaceutical, and food industries for their ability to prevent contamination and promote efficient processing.</p></p>
