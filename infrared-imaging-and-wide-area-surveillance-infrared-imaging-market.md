<p><p>Infrared Imaging and Wide Area Surveillance encompasses various infrared spectrum technologies used for thermal detection and surveillance. The Near IR (NIR) range focuses on detecting short wavelengths, ideal for low-light conditions and enhanced imaging. Short Wave IR (SWIR) captures images in slightly longer wavelengths, offering better penetration through atmospheric conditions. Mid Wave IR (MWIR) is effective for thermal imaging, providing details in varied temperatures. Long Wave IR (LWIR) specializes in detecting heat, making it crucial for night vision and surveillance applications.</p></p>
