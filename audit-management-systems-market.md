<p><p>Audit Management Systems are categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems facilitate remote access and scalability, allowing organizations to manage audits from anywhere while reducing IT maintenance costs. They typically offer subscription models and automatic updates. Conversely, on-premises solutions are installed locally on an organization's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more significant upfront investments and ongoing maintenance efforts. Both types aim to enhance the efficiency and compliance of audit processes.</p></p>
