<p><p>The linear CCD image sensor market consists primarily of two types: monochrome linear CMOS image sensors and color linear CMOS image sensors. Monochrome linear CMOS sensors capture images in grayscale, making them ideal for high-contrast applications such as barcode scanning and industrial inspection where color information is unnecessary. In contrast, color linear CMOS image sensors record images in full color, suitable for applications requiring detailed color differentiation, such as document scanning, medical imaging, and machine vision, enhancing overall image analysis.</p></p>
