<p><p>The car steering systems market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical and power steering systems. Mechanical steering systems rely on a direct linkage between the steering wheel and the wheels, providing a more traditional and tactile driving experience. In contrast, power steering systems use hydraulic or electric assistance to reduce the effort needed to turn the wheel, enhancing maneuverability and comfort. This shift towards power steering has gained popularity due to its ease of use, especially in larger or heavier vehicles.</p></p>
