<p><p>The **Solar Rooftop Market** is categorized into **On-Grid** and **Off-Grid** systems. **On-Grid** systems are connected to the public electricity grid, allowing excess energy to be fed back, often earning credits or reducing bills. They rely on the grid for backup when solar power is insufficient. **Off-Grid** systems operate independently, using batteries to store energy for use during non-sunny periods. They are ideal for remote areas without grid access, providing self-sufficiency but requiring higher upfront costs for storage solutions.</p></p>
