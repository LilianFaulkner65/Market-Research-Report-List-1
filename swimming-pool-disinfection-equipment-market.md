<p><p>The swimming pool disinfection equipment market includes various types designed to maintain water quality. Chlorine dosing equipment adds chlorine to eliminate pathogens. Ozonation disinfection equipment uses ozone gas to disinfect and oxidize impurities. Ultraviolet (UV) disinfection equipment employs UV light to inactivate microorganisms without chemicals. Electrolysis disinfection equipment generates chlorine through electrolysis from saltwater. Other equipment may include alternative disinfection methods like ionization and various chemical formulations, catering to diverse pool maintenance needs and preferences.</p></p>
