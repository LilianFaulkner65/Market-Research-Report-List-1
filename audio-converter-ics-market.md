<p><p>The audio converter IC market is primarily divided into two types: audio A/D (analog-to-digital) converter ICs and audio D/A (digital-to-analog) converter ICs. A/D converter ICs transform analog audio signals into digital format, allowing for processing and digital transmission, essential in recording and communication devices. Conversely, D/A converter ICs convert digital audio data back into analog signals for playback through speakers or amplifiers, making them crucial in consumer electronics and professional audio equipment. Together, these ICs enhance audio quality and versatility in various applications.</p></p>
