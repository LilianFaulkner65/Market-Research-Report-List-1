<p><p>The automotive cabin AC filter market comprises three main types: electrostatic filters, particulate filters, and charcoal filters. Electrostatic filters use static electricity to attract and capture airborne particles, improving air quality. Particulate filters focus on trapping dust, pollen, and other particles to enhance cabin comfort. Charcoal filters incorporate activated carbon to absorb odors and harmful gases, providing a fresher atmosphere within the vehicle. Each type serves to improve air quality and passenger comfort in varying ways, addressing specific environmental concerns.</p></p>
