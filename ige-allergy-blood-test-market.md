<p><p>The IgE Allergy Blood Test market comprises several testing methods that measure allergen-specific IgE antibodies in the bloodstream. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) testing is a commonly used format due to its simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Fluorescent Enzyme Immunoassays (FEIA) utilize fluorescent labels for increased sensitivity and specificity in detecting allergens. Chemiluminescent Immunoassays employ luminous reactions to enhance detection capabilities, offering rapid and accurate results. Each method caters to varying laboratory capacities and clinical needs in allergy diagnosis.</p></p>
