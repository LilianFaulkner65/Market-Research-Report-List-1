<p><p>The telecentric machine vision lens market comprises various types, including Object Square Telephoto Lenses and Bi-Telecentric Lenses. Object Square Telephoto Lenses maintain a consistent object size at varying distances, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision. Bi-Telecentric Lenses, on the other hand, ensure uniform magnification and minimize perspective errors, enabling accurate measurements across a wide field of view. Both types are crucial for industrial automation, quality control, and advanced imaging systems, enhancing measurement reliability and image quality.</p></p>
