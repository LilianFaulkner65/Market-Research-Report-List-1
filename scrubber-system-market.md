<p><strong>The Scrubber System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The scrubber system market comprises various types, including open-loop scrubbers, which use seawater for wash water and discharge treated effluent back to the ocean; closed-loop scrubbers, which recirculate water with added chemicals to neutralize sulfur; hybrid scrubbers, offering flexibility between open and closed-loop operations; dry scrubbers, using dry reagents to capture pollutants; and membrane scrubbers, employing membranes to separate pollutants from emissions. Each type addresses compliance with environmental regulations while minimizing operational impact.</p></p>
