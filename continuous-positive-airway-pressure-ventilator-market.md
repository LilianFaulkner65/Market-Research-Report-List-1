<p><strong>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-CPAP</li><li>Auto CPAP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) ventilator market is primarily divided into two types: Fixed CPAP and Auto CPAP. Fixed CPAP delivers a consistent, preset pressure throughout the night, ensuring a steady airflow to keep airways open. In contrast, Auto CPAP dynamically adjusts the pressure in response to the user's needs, providing varying levels of support based on real-time monitoring of breathing patterns. This adaptability makes Auto CPAP suitable for a wider range of sleep apnea severity levels.</p></p>
