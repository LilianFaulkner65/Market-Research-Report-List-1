<p><p>The Personal Radiation Monitor market comprises two main types: active and passive monitors. Active type monitors continuously measure and display radiation levels in real-time, often incorporating alarms for immediate alerts, making them suitable for high-risk environments. In contrast, passive type monitors, such as film badges, accumulate radiation exposure over time without constant monitoring, providing a record for later analysis. Both types serve vital roles in ensuring safety and compliance in various fields, including healthcare, nuclear energy, and research.</p></p>
