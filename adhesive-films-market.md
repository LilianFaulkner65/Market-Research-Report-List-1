<p><p>The adhesive films market is categorized into several types, including pressure-sensitive, hot-melt, light-cured, and others. Pressure-sensitive films bond quickly upon contact without requiring additional heat or solvents. Hot-melt films are applied in liquid form and solidify upon cooling, offering strong adhesion. Light-cured films utilize ultraviolet light to initiate curing, providing fast processing times. Other types encompass a range of specialized adhesives designed for niche applications. Each type serves distinct industries, ensuring versatile bonding solutions across various sectors.</p></p>
