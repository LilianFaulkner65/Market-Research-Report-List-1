<p><p>Cell and gene therapy manufacturing QC encompasses several critical testing types to ensure product safety and efficacy. Safety testing evaluates potential adverse effects to ensure patient safety. Purity testing confirms the absence of contaminants, verifying the sample's quality. Potency testing assesses the therapeutic effect, ensuring the product delivers the intended action. Identity testing determines the correct biological material is present. Stability testing evaluates the product's shelf life and effectiveness under various conditions, ensuring reliability over time. Together, these tests safeguard therapeutic outcomes.</p></p>
