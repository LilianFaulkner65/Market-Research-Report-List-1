<p><ul><li>Solar Control</li><li>Convection Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car glass insulation film market includes two main types: solar control and convection control films. Solar control films are designed to reduce heat and glare from sunlight, enhancing passenger comfort and protecting interiors from UV damage. In contrast, convection control films focus on minimizing heat transfer through the glass, improving energy efficiency by keeping the vehicle cooler in hot weather and warmer in cold conditions. Both types enhance driving comfort and contribute to vehicle sustainability.</p></p>
