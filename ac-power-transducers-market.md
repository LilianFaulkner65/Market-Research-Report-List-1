Dual-phase transducers find use in split-phase systems, commonly in small commercial settings. Multi-phase transducers, capable of monitoring three-phase systems, cater to industrial applications, enabling precise energy management and improved performance in complex electrical networks. Each type addresses specific application requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
