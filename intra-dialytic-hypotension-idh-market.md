<p><p>Intra Dialytic Hypotension (IDH) is a condition characterized by a significant drop in blood pressure that occurs during dialysis treatment. It is a common complication for patients with chronic kidney disease receiving hemodialysis, leading to adverse outcomes such as nausea, vomiting, and cardiovascular complications. The increasing prevalence of end-stage renal disease (ESRD) and the rising number of patients requiring dialysis are key drivers for the IDH market. </p><p>Recent trends indicate a growing emphasis on improving dialysis techniques and technologies to minimize IDH incidents. Innovations such as biofeedback systems, advanced monitoring devices, and fluid management solutions are being developed to enhance patient experience and safety. Furthermore, the increasing awareness of optimized dialysis protocols contributes to market growth.</p><p>The Intra Dialytic Hypotension IDH Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.10% during the forecast period.
