<p><p>The semiconductor butterfly valve market comprises various types, primarily distinguished by materials such as SS304 and SS316L. SS304, a versatile stainless steel, is commonly used in applications requiring good corrosion resistance and strength, making it suitable for general environments. SS316L, with added molybdenum, offers superior resistance to chloride-induced corrosion, ideal for harsh chemical processes in semiconductor manufacturing. Both materials cater to the industry's stringent requirements for precision, reliability, and durability in controlling fluid flow within semiconductor production systems.</p></p>
