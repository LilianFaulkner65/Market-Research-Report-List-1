<p><p>The heart valve retractors market includes two primary types: sliding and rotating. In contrast, rotating type retractors enable 360-degree movement, providing enhanced maneuverability and visibility in complex surgical environments. Both types play a crucial role in minimizing tissue trauma and improving surgical outcomes in cardiac procedures.</p></p>
