<p><p>The azo polymerization initiator market can be categorized into water-soluble and oil-soluble types. Water-soluble azo initiators are designed for applications in aqueous environments, often used in water-based coatings and adhesives, providing efficient polymerization without affecting the system's stability. In contrast, oil-soluble azo initiators are utilized in non-aqueous environments, suitable for applications like paints, inks, and plastics, ensuring effective polymer formation in oil-based systems.
