<p><p>Parking lot sweepers are specialized vehicles designed to clean large paved areas. **Mechanical sweepers** use rotating brushes to sweep debris into a hopper, ideal for heavy materials like gravel and leaves. **Vacuum air sweepers** employ powerful suction to collect fine dust and lightweight debris, suitable for areas requiring minimal dust dispersion. **Regenerative air sweepers** combine air circulation and filtration systems to capture fine particles efficiently, making them effective for dust-sensitive environments. Each type caters to specific cleaning needs, ensuring optimal performance in diverse parking lot conditions.</p></p>
