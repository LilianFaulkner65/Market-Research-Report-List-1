<p><p>The Polyethylene (PE) foams market is categorized into two main types: Non-XLPE (Non-Cross-Linked Polyethylene) foam and XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) foam. Non-XLPE foam is characterized by its softness and flexibility, making it suitable for applications like packaging and cushioning. In contrast, XLPE foam features a more rigid structure due to cross-linking, enhancing its durability, chemical resistance, and thermal insulation properties, which are ideal for automotive, construction, and protective packaging applications. Both types cater to specific industry needs.</p></p>
