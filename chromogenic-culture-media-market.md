<p><p>Chromogenic culture media is categorized into three types: natural medium, mixed medium, and semi-composite media. Natural medium contains nutrients derived from plant or animal sources, promoting the growth of specific microorganisms. Mixed medium combines various substrates to support a diverse range of organisms, enhancing detection accuracy. Semi-composite media blends natural and synthetic components, providing a balanced environment that fosters selective growth while facilitating the identification of target bacteria. These media types are essential for microbiological diagnostics and research applications.</p></p>
