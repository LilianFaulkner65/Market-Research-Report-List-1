<p><p>Applanation Type Tonometers are specialized devices used in ophthalmology to measure intraocular pressure (IOP), which is crucial for diagnosing and managing conditions like glaucoma. These tonometers work by flattening a small area of the cornea and measuring the force required to achieve this flattening. The accuracy and reliability of applanation tonometry have made it the gold standard in IOP measurement, contributing to increased adoption in clinical practice.</p><p>The Applanation Type Tonometers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.80% during the forecast period.
