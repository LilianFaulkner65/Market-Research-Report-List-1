<p><strong>The Viewfinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Viewfinder</li><li>Electronic Viewfinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The viewfinder market is primarily divided into two types: optical viewfinders (OVFs) and electronic viewfinders (EVFs). OVFs use a series of lenses and mirrors to provide a direct optical image of the scene, favored for their real-time, lag-free performance and natural view. In contrast, EVFs rely on electronic displays to show a digital representation of the scene, allowing for features like exposure previews and focus peaking. Each type caters to different user preferences and photography styles, driving distinct market segments.</p></p>
