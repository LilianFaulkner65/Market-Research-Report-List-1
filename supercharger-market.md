<p><strong>The Supercharger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roots Supercharger</li><li>Twin-Screw Supercharger</li><li>Centrifugal Supercharger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The supercharger market is segmented into three main types: Roots, Twin-Screw, and Centrifugal superchargers. </p><p>Roots superchargers utilize two rotors to compress air, providing immediate power but lower efficiency. Twin-screw superchargers offer improved efficiency and higher boost at higher speeds, using interlocking rotors to compress air. Centrifugal superchargers, resembling a turbocharger, use a spinning impeller to generate boost, offering a compact solution with efficiency at various RPMs. Each type caters to specific performance needs in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
