<p><p>A tilt rotor aircraft is a hybrid vehicle that combines the vertical takeoff and landing (VTOL) capabilities of a helicopter with the speed and range of a fixed-wing airplane. It achieves this by using rotors that can tilt between vertical and horizontal positions, enabling it to transition seamlessly between hovering and forward flight. This unique design makes tilt rotor aircraft highly versatile for applications in military, commercial, and emergency services.</p><p>The Tilt Rotor Aircraft Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.6% during the forecast period.
