<p><p>The automotive refinish market encompasses several types of coatings used for vehicle repair and maintenance. Solvent-borne paints are traditional formulations that use organic solvents as a base, delivering high durability and gloss but with environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. Water-borne coatings, in contrast, utilize water as a primary solvent, offering lower emissions and improved safety, making them increasingly popular. Other market segments include powder coatings and UV-cured paints, which provide unique benefits like superior finish and reduced environmental impact.</p></p>
