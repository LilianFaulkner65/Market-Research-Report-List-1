<p><p>The medical X-ray film processor market is categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic film processors. Semi-automatic film processors require manual intervention for various steps, such as loading and unloading films, while automating the processing itself, making them suitable for smaller facilities with moderate workloads. In contrast, fully automatic film processors perform all processing steps without human intervention, offering increased efficiency and consistency, making them ideal for high-volume imaging centers and hospitals. Both types cater to different operational needs.</p></p>
