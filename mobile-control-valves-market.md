<p><p>The mobile control valves market comprises two primary types: monoblock valves and sectional valves. Monoblock valves are compact, featuring a single block design that integrates multiple functions, making them ideal for applications with space constraints. Sectional valves, on the other hand, consist of multiple interconnected sections that allow for greater flexibility and scalability in controlling hydraulic systems. Each type serves distinct needs in mobile machinery, enhancing operational efficiency and control in various industrial applications.</p></p>
