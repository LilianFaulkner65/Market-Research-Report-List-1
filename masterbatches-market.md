<p><p>The masterbatch market comprises various types, each serving specific functions. White masterbatch provides opacity and brightness to plastics, while black masterbatch enhances UV stability and pigmentation. Color masterbatch offers a wide spectrum of hues for customized coloring in products. Additive masterbatch incorporates specific properties like antiblocking and antioxidant features to enhance plastic properties. Lastly, plastic filler masterbatch reduces costs and improves material bulk by incorporating fillers like calcium carbonate or talc, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
