<p><p>The rolling lubricant market consists of three key types: synthetic, semi-synthetic, and mineral. Synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered for superior performance, offering enhanced protection and stability under extreme conditions. Semi-synthetic lubricants combine both synthetic and mineral oils, providing a balanced performance, cost-effectiveness, and improved lubrication properties. Mineral lubricants, derived from refined crude oil, are widely used due to their affordability and availability, though they generally offer lower performance compared to synthetic options. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The rolling lubricant market caters to both hot and cold rolling processes in metalworking.
