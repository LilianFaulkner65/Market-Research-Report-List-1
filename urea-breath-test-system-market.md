<p><p>The Urea Breath Test System market is primarily categorized into two technology types: Scintillation Technology and Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometry (IRMS). Scintillation Technology employs a radioactive isotope to detect urea in breath samples, providing rapid results for diagnosing Helicobacter pylori infections. In contrast, IRMS offers a more detailed analysis by measuring the isotopic composition of carbon dioxide in breath samples, delivering high sensitivity and specificity. Both technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy, supporting effective treatment decisions for gastrointestinal disorders.</p></p>
