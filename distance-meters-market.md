<p><ul><li>Electro-Optical Distance Meters</li><li>Laser Distance Meters</li><li>Sound Wave Distance Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distance meters market comprises various types, each utilizing distinct technologies for measuring distances. Electro-optical distance meters use light pulses and their reflection to calculate distance accurately. Laser distance meters employ laser beams for precise measurements over long distances, making them ideal for construction and surveying. Sound wave distance meters utilize sound waves, typically in ultrasonic form, to gauge distances, often used in home improvement and general applications. Each type serves specific needs across different industries, enhancing measurement accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
