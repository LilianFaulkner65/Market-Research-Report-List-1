<p><p>The drone delivery service market is categorized into three types based on range: close range, short range, and mid-range drones. Close range drones are designed for very small delivery distances, typically within a few kilometers, ideal for local deliveries. Short range drones cover moderate distances, generally up to 25 kilometers, suitable for urban and suburban areas. Mid-range drones can travel further, usually between 25 to 100 kilometers, enabling deliveries to regional hubs or remote locations, enhancing logistics flexibility.</p></p>
