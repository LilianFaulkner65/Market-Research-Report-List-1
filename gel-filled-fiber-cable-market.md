<p><p>Gel-filled fiber cables utilize a gel substance to protect the fiber from moisture, enhancing durability and performance. The market is divided into two main types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode fibers. Single-Mode fibers, characterized by a single light path, are ideal for long-distance communication due to their minimal signal loss. Multi-Mode fibers, featuring multiple light paths, are suited for shorter distances and are commonly used in local area networks. Both types cater to different networking needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
