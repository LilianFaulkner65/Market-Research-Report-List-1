<p><p>The waterjet cutting abrasive market is categorized primarily into four types: silica, alumina, garnet, and others. Silica is cost-effective but less efficient for high-performance cuts. Alumina offers higher durability and wear resistance, making it suitable for tough materials. Garnet is the most widely used due to its superior cutting capabilities and minimal environmental impact. Other abrasives may include variations like magnesium oxide or copper slag, catering to specific applications based on material properties and cutting requirements.</p></p>
