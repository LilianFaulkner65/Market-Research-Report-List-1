<p><ul><li>Squeeze Type</li><li>Pump Type</li><li>Pen Type</li><li>Gravity Leakage Type</li><li>Suction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor water filter market encompasses various types designed for portability and ease of use. Squeeze type filters require users to squeeze the bag to push water through the filter. Pump type filters operate by manually pumping water through the system, offering faster filtration. Pen type filters are compact and suitable for individual use, filtering small amounts directly. Gravity leakage type filters rely on gravity to pull water through, while suction type filters enable users to draw water from a source directly. Each type addresses specific outdoor recreational needs.</p></p>
