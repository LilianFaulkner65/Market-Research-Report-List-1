<p><p>The Self-Healing Paint Protection Film market is categorized into three main types: PVC, PU, and TPU. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) films are cost-effective and provide basic protection, though they may lack flexibility. PU (Polyurethane) films offer increased durability and self-healing properties, allowing minor scratches to disappear when exposed to heat. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) films combine the advantages of both PVC and PU, providing superior elasticity, abrasion resistance, and advanced self-healing features, making them ideal for high-performance applications.</p></p>
