Open Type lifeboats are designed for quick deployment and easy evacuation but provide less protection. Partially Enclosed Type offers a mix of coverage and accessibility, enhancing safety during rough conditions. Fully Enclosed Type lifeboats provide maximum protection from the elements, ensuring crew safety in severe weather. Each type serves specific operational requirements and safety standards in maritime environments.</p></p>
