<p><p>Hotel accounting software is divided into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software operates over the internet, allowing for real-time access from multiple devices and facilitating remote management. It typically offers subscription-based pricing and automatic updates. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed directly on hotel servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring upfront investment and maintenance. Both types cater to different operational needs and preferences in the hospitality industry.</p></p>
