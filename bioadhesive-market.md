<p><p>The bioadhesive market is primarily categorized into plant-based and animal-based bioadhesives. Plant-based bioadhesives are derived from natural sources like starches, gums, and cellulose, offering sustainable and biodegradable options for various applications, including packaging and construction. In contrast, animal-based bioadhesives are sourced from proteins and other materials from animals, often utilized in specialized applications like woodworking and medical adhesives due to their strong bonding properties. Both types contribute to eco-friendly alternatives in adhesive technologies.</p></p>
