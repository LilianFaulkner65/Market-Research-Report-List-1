<p><strong>The Automotive Braking Energy Recovery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ESC Based System</li><li>ABS Based System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive braking energy recovery systems capture and store kinetic energy during braking to enhance vehicle efficiency. The ESC (Electronic Stability Control) based system utilizes stability control mechanisms to optimize energy recovery while maintaining vehicle stability. ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) based systems focus on preventing wheel lock-up during hard braking, allowing energy recovery without compromising safety. Other systems may incorporate innovative technologies such as regenerative braking in hybrids and electric vehicles, further improving energy efficiency and reducing emissions.</p></p>
