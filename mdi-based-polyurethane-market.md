<p><strong>The MDI Based Polyurethane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure MDI</li><li>Polymeric MDI (PMDI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MDI-based polyurethane market includes two primary types: Pure MDI and Polymeric MDI (PMDI). Pure MDI, or diphenylmethane diisocyanate, is a liquid form used in high-performance applications like elastomers, adhesives, and coatings due to its superior strength and flexibility. Polymeric MDI (PMDI), a mixture of MDI isomers, is a viscous liquid primarily used in rigid foam production for insulation, construction, and automotive sectors. Both types are key raw materials in polyurethane manufacturing, catering to diverse industrial needs based on their unique properties.</p></p>
