<p><p>The Airway Management Resuscitation Devices market includes various essential tools for maintaining airway patency during resuscitation. Ventilators provide mechanical breathing support, ensuring adequate oxygenation. Endotracheal tubes secure the airway directly in the trachea, while laryngeal mask airways offer a less invasive alternative to ventilate patients. Nasopharyngeal airways facilitate airway access through the nose, and oropharyngeal airways prevent airway obstruction by keeping the tongue away from the throat. Together, these devices are vital in emergency and critical care settings.</p></p>
