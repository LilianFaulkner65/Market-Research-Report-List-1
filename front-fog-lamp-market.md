<p><p>The front fog lamp market is segmented into three main types: LED, Xenon, and Halogen. LED fog lamps are favored for their energy efficiency and long lifespan, providing bright illumination with low power consumption. Xenon lamps deliver intense brightness and a unique color temperature, enhancing visibility in adverse weather conditions but require a more complex installation. Halogen lamps are the traditional choice, offering affordability and easy replacement, though they tend to have a shorter lifespan and lower brightness compared to the other types.</p></p>
