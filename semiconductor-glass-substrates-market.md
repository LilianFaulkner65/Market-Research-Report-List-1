<p><p>The semiconductor glass substrates market is primarily divided into two types: alkali glass and alkali-free glass. Alkali glass, containing elements like sodium and potassium, is favored for its favorable thermal and optical properties, making it ideal for various electronic applications. Conversely, alkali-free glass, which omits these alkali metals, provides enhanced chemical resistance and lower levels of ionic conductivity, making it suitable for high-performance semiconductor devices. Both types cater to the advanced needs of the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
