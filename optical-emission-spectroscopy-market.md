<p><p>The Optical Emission Spectroscopy (OES) market is primarily divided into two types: Arc/Spark OES and Inductively Coupled Plasma (ICP) OES. Arc/Spark OES uses high-energy electrical arcs or sparks to excite atoms in a sample, making it suitable for metals and alloys analysis. In contrast, ICP OES employs an inductively coupled plasma to ionize the sample, allowing for the detection of trace elements in various materials. Both methods are valuable for elemental analysis in industries like metallurgy, environmental testing, and materials science.</p></p>
