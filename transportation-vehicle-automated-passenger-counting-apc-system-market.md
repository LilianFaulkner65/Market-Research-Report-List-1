<p><p>The Transportation Vehicle Automated Passenger Counting (APC) System market includes several technologies to track passenger numbers. The Infrared Type uses infrared sensors to detect movements across a plane, counting passengers as they enter or exit. The Time-of-Flight Type measures the time it takes for light signals to reflect off passengers, providing high accuracy. The Stereoscopic Vision Type employs dual cameras to create 3D images, allowing for precise counting even in crowded spaces. Each technology enhances operational efficiency and data collection in public transport.</p></p>
