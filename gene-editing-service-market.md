<p><strong>The Gene Editing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ex-Vivo</li><li>In-Vivo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gene editing service market is primarily categorized into two types: ex-vivo and in-vivo. Ex-vivo gene editing involves altering genes outside the body, where cells are extracted, modified, and then reintroduced, commonly used in treatments like CAR-T cell therapy. In-vivo gene editing occurs directly within the organism, targeting specific cells or tissues, typically utilizing delivery methods such as viral vectors. Both approaches are essential for therapeutic applications, research, and potential advancements in personalized medicine.</p></p>
