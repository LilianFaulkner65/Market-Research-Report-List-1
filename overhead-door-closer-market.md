<p><p>The overhead door closer market is categorized into single action and double action types. Single action door closers operate in one direction, allowing the door to close automatically after opening, ideal for one-way access. In contrast, double action door closers enable doors to swing in both directions and return to the closed position, accommodating high traffic areas. These systems enhance convenience and security in various commercial and residential applications, promoting efficient door operation and durability.</p></p>
