<p><p>The amphibious vehicle market offers various propulsion types to enhance versatility across land and water. Screw propeller propulsion utilizes rotating blades to push vehicles through water, ideal for smooth sailing and efficient movement. Water jet propulsion employs powerful jets of water for swift maneuverability, enhancing speed and agility in aquatic environments. Track-based propulsion combines traditional vehicle tracks with buoyancy, allowing seamless transitions over soft terrains while maintaining stability in water. These propulsion methods cater to diverse operational needs and environments.</p></p>
