<p><ul><li>Fluorescence detection</li><li>Magnetic detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-checkout counters can utilize different detection technologies to prevent theft and enhance efficiency. Fluorescence detection employs UV light to identify specific markings or security tags that fluoresce, ensuring items are correctly scanned. On the other hand, magnetic detection uses magnetic strips embedded in products to trigger alarms when not properly processed. Both systems aim to streamline the checkout process while minimizing loss, catering to varying retail environments and customer preferences in the evolving market landscape.</p></p>
