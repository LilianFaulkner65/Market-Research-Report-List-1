<p><strong>The Wood Pellets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>White Pellet</li><li>Black Pellet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood pellets market primarily consists of two types: white pellets and black pellets. White pellets, produced from clean, untreated wood biomass, are predominantly used for residential heating and power generation due to their high energy efficiency and minimal emissions. In contrast, black pellets are made from whole biomass, including lignin, and undergo a torrefaction process, enhancing their energy density and making them suitable for industrial applications. Both types support renewable energy initiatives but cater to different market segments and end-user needs.</p></p>
