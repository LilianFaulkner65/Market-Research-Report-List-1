<p><p>The toner market is primarily divided into traditional toner and chemical toner types. Traditional toner, often used in laser printers, consists of fine powder made from pigments and polymers that adhere to paper through heat and pressure, ensuring vibrant print quality. In contrast, chemical toner is produced through a more advanced process, allowing for smaller particle sizes and improved performance. This type offers better color accuracy and faster print speeds, catering to high-demand environments like commercial printing and professional design.</p></p>
