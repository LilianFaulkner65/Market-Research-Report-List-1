<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing Equipment (NDTE) encompasses various technologies used to evaluate materials without causing damage. Key market types include:</p><p>1. **Ultrasonic Testing Equipment**: Uses high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws.</p><p>2. **Magnetic Particle Testing Equipment**: Identifies surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials using magnetic fields and iron particles.</p><p>3. **Visual Inspection Equipment**: Involves direct observation aided by tools like cameras or magnifiers.</p><p>4. **Radiography Testing Equipment**: Utilizes X-rays or gamma rays to reveal internal structures.</p><p>5. **Penetrant Testing Equipment**: Detects surface-breaking defects using dyes.</p><p>6. **Eddy Current Testing Equipment**: Measures conductivity changes to find flaws in conductive materials.</p><p>7. **Acoustic Emission Testing Equipment**: Monitors sound waves emitted by materials under stress to identify defects.</p></p>
