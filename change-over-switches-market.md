<p><p>Changeover switches are essential devices used to switch between different power sources or circuits. The two primary market types are automatic and manual changeover switches. Automatic changeover switches operate without human intervention, seamlessly transitioning between power sources during outages or fluctuations, ensuring continuity. In contrast, manual changeover switches require user input to select the power source, offering greater control but necessitating user awareness and action during power changes. Each type serves specific needs within various applications in electrical systems.</p></p>
