<p><p>The No-clean Flux Solder Paste market features two primary types: Leaded and Lead-free solder paste. Leaded solder paste contains tin and lead, providing excellent wetting properties, making it suitable for various applications but facing restrictions due to health concerns. In contrast, Lead-free solder paste replaces lead with alternatives like silver or copper, aligning with environmental regulations and offering improved safety but often requiring higher processing temperatures. Both types play crucial roles in electronics manufacturing, catering to different industry requirements.</p></p>
