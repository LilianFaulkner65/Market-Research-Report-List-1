<p><p>The 2019 nCoV testing kit market comprises two primary types: Fluorescent PCR and Colloidal Gold tests. Fluorescent PCR tests utilize polymerase chain reaction technology to amplify viral genetic material, providing rapid and highly accurate results, making them essential for clinical diagnostics. In contrast, Colloidal Gold tests are rapid, easy-to-use kits that utilize antibodies to detect viral antigens, offering quick results, often in field settings. Both types play a crucial role in the effective identification and management of COVID-19 cases.</p></p>
