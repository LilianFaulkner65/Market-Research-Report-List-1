<p><p>MEMS Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs) are categorized based on the degrees of freedom (DoF) they provide in motion sensing. </p><p>- **4DoF** includes accelerometers and gyroscopes for basic motion tracking.  </p><p>- **6DoF** adds magnetometers for orientation in three-dimensional space.  </p><p>- **9DoF** incorporates additional sensors like pressure sensors for more comprehensive motion and altitude data.  </p><p>- **10DoF** further enhances capabilities with augmented sensors, enabling advanced applications.  </p><p>- **Other** includes specialized configurations catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
