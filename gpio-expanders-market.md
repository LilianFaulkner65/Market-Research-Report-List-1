<p><p>The GPIO expander market encompasses various types, including quasi-bidirectional GPIO, LED PWM, and open-drain GPIO. Quasi-bidirectional GPIO allows pins to function in both input and output modes, enhancing flexibility. LED PWM expanders are designed for precise control of LED brightness through pulse-width modulation, catering to display and lighting applications. Open-drain GPIO is essential for managing multiple devices on a single line, allowing for wired logic configurations. Together, these types enable diverse applications in embedded systems, IoT, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
