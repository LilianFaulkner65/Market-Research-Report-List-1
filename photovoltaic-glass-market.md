<p><strong>The Photovoltaic Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Poly-Crystalline</li><li>Mono-Crystalline</li><li>Thin Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic glass market comprises three main types: Poly-Crystalline, Mono-Crystalline, and Thin Film. Poly-Crystalline solar cells are made from multiple silicon crystals, offering a balance of efficiency and cost, making them popular for various applications. Mono-Crystalline cells, composed of single silicon crystals, provide higher efficiency and space-saving solutions, often used in residential and commercial installations. Thin Film technology, which utilizes a variety of materials, is flexible and lightweight, suited for large-area applications and building-integrated photovoltaics.</p></p>
