<p><strong>The Optical Fingerprint Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multispectral Imaging</li><li>Electro optical Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fingerprint sensor market is categorized into two main types: multispectral imaging and electro-optical imaging. Multispectral imaging captures various wavelengths of light, allowing for enhanced detail and the ability to read fingerprints under challenging conditions, such as dirt or moisture. In contrast, electro-optical imaging utilizes a combination of light and electronic sensing technology to create high-resolution images of fingerprints. Both technologies enhance fingerprint recognition accuracy and security in applications like smartphones and biometric systems.</p></p>
