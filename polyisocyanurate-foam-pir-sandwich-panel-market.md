<p><p>Polyisocyanurate Foam (PIR) Sandwich Panels are composite building materials consisting of a core made from polyisocyanurate foam sandwiched between two rigid facings, typically made of metal or other materials. They are widely used in construction for insulation due to their excellent thermal resistance, lightweight nature, and cost-effectiveness. These panels are particularly popular in cold storage facilities, industrial applications, and commercial buildings, owing to their superior energy efficiency and ability to meet stringent building codes.</p><p>The Polyisocyanurate Foam (PIR) Sandwich Panel Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient building materials and stringent regulations on energy conservation. Innovations such as improved fire resistance and enhanced mechanical performance are also influencing market dynamics.
