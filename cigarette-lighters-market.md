<p><p>The cigarette lighters market is categorized into three main types: liquid lighters, gaseous lighters, and gas-liquid mixing lighters. Liquid lighters utilize liquid fuel, often butane, for ignition, providing a steady flame. Gaseous lighters, typically using propane or butane gas, produce a flame instantly upon ignition. Gas-liquid mixing lighters combine both liquid and gas mechanisms, enabling efficient ignition and flame control. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and offers varying features, portability, and ease of use.</p></p>
