<p><p>The automotive printed circuit board (PCB) market consists of three main types: single-sided, double-sided, and multi-layer PCBs. Single-sided PCBs have components mounted on one side, making them cost-effective for simple applications. Double-sided PCBs allow for components on both sides, supporting more complex designs without increasing size. Multi-layer PCBs, featuring multiple layers of circuitry, enable high-density interconnections and are essential for advanced automotive systems like infotainment and safety features, driving demand for innovation and efficiency in automotive electronics.</p></p>
