<p><p>Ion milling systems are critical in materials analysis and fabrication, particularly in semiconductor and nanotechnology sectors. The market consists of three primary types: Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM) utilize focused electron beams to generate high-resolution images of surface topographies. Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM) allow for ultra-precise imaging and analysis at atomic resolutions by transmitting electrons through ultra-thin samples. Focused Ion Beams (FIB) are used for material modification and nanomachining, providing detailed insights and customization capabilities for advanced materials research.</p></p>
