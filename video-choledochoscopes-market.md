<p><p>The video choledochoscope market is segmented into rigid and flexible choledochoscopes. Rigid choledochoscopes are designed for straight-line access, providing clear visualization during procedures but limited maneuverability. They are often used in straightforward cases. In contrast, flexible choledochoscopes offer enhanced articulation and movement, making them suitable for complex intrahepatic or extrahepatic bile duct access. Both types are crucial for diagnosing and treating biliary tract conditions, with the choice depending on the specific clinical requirements and anatomical considerations.</p></p>
