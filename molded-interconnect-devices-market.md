<p><p>Molded Interconnect Devices (MID) encompass various manufacturing techniques for integrating electronic circuits directly into three-dimensional plastic components. Laser Direct Structuring (LDS) utilizes laser methods to define circuit paths on plastic substrates, allowing for intricate designs. Two-Shot Molding involves injecting two different materials sequentially, enabling the integration of conductive and non-conductive elements into a single part. Other methods may include embossing or stamping techniques. Together, these approaches enhance design flexibility, reduce assembly costs, and improve the performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
