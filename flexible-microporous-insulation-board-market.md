<p><p>Flexible Microporous Insulation Board market includes two primary types: Alumina Silica Material and Calcium Magnesium Silicate Material. Alumina Silica boards offer excellent thermal resistance and low thermal conductivity, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. On the other hand, Calcium Magnesium Silicate boards provide good thermal insulation and are often used in industrial applications due to their fire-resistant properties. Both materials are lightweight and flexible, making them suitable for various insulation needs across industries like construction, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
