<p><p>The fiber-coupled electro-optical light modulators market is composed of two primary types: electro-optical intensity modulators and electro-optical phase modulators. Intensity modulators control the amplitude of light signals, making them crucial for transmitting varying signal strengths in communication systems. Conversely, phase modulators manipulate the phase of light waves, enabling advanced applications in quantum optics and coherent communication. Both types are integral for enhancing data transmission capabilities in fiber optics, supporting a wide range of telecommunications and photonic applications.</p></p>
