<p><p>The Child Resistant Closures market comprises two main types: reclosable and non-reclosable. Reclosable closures can be securely resealed after opening, providing convenience and safety for products that may require repeated access, such as pharmaceuticals or household chemicals. Non-reclosable closures, on the other hand, are designed for single-use and do not allow resealing, ensuring that once opened, the contents are not easily accessible again, enhancing safety for hazardous substances. Both types aim to prevent accidental ingestion by children.</p></p>
