<p><p>Vertical Surge Tanks (VST) are essential in hydraulic systems to manage pressure fluctuations caused by rapid changes in flow. The market for VSTs is categorized into Single Compartment and Twin Compartment types. Single Compartment VSTs consist of a single chamber that accommodates pressure changes, making them ideal for smaller systems. In contrast, Twin Compartment VSTs feature two chambers, allowing for better pressure regulation and energy dissipation, making them suitable for larger and more complex hydraulic systems.</p></p>
