<p><p>The industrial dryers market is categorized into three main types: direct dryers, indirect dryers, and specialty dryers. Direct dryers operate by exposing materials to heated air or gas, facilitating rapid evaporation. Indirect dryers rely on heat conduction, transferring heat through a wall without direct contact with the drying medium. Specialty dryers are tailored for specific applications or materials, addressing unique drying challenges across various industries, including food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemical manufacturing, ensuring efficient moisture removal and product quality.</p></p>
