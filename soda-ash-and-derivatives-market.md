<p><p>Soda ash, or sodium carbonate, is classified into two primary market types: dense soda ash and light soda ash. Dense soda ash is characterized by its higher bulk density, making it suitable for glass manufacturing, detergents, and various industrial applications requiring efficient transport and storage. Light soda ash, with a lower bulk density, is mainly used in the production of chemicals, textiles, and in water treatment processes. Both types serve distinct roles in diverse sectors, reflecting their unique physical properties.</p></p>
