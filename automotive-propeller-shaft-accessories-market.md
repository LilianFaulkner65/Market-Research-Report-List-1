<p><p>The automotive propeller shaft accessories market includes key components like slip joints and universal joints. Slip joints allow axial movement, accommodating changes in shaft length due to suspension travel, ensuring smooth power transmission. Universal joints enable angular movement, connecting shafts at varying angles while maintaining rotational motion. Both are critical for efficient torque transfer in vehicles, especially in drivetrains with complex geometries. These accessories enhance durability, reduce vibrations, and improve overall vehicle performance, catering to diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
