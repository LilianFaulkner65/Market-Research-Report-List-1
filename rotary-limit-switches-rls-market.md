The market is primarily divided into SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) switches, which allow a single input to control multiple outputs, and DPDT (Double Pole Double Throw) switches, capable of handling two independent circuits simultaneously. Additionally, there are other switch types that cater to specific applications. These switches ensure operational safety and efficiency in various industrial automation and mechanical processes.</p></p>
