Novolac epoxy resin coatings excel in high-temperature and chemical resistance, often used in harsh environments. Aliphatic epoxy resin coatings are UV-resistant, making them perfect for outdoor applications. Other types cater to specialized needs, offering unique properties like flexibility or enhanced durability.</p></p>
