<p><p>The residential gas alarm market is divided into two main types: stationary gas alarms and portable gas alarms. Stationary gas alarms are fixed installations designed to continuously monitor specific areas for hazardous gas levels, providing alerts for residents. They are typically integrated into home safety systems for reliable, 24/7 coverage.
