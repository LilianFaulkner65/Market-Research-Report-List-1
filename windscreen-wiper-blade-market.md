<p><p>The windscreen wiper blade market is categorized into three main types: boneless, bone, and hybrid. Boneless blades feature a sleek design without the traditional metal frame, providing improved aerodynamics and a quiet operation. Bone blades use a metal framework for durability and reliable performance, making them a popular choice. Hybrid blades combine the benefits of both styles, offering a design that enhances visibility and reduces noise while maintaining strength and flexibility, catering to diverse consumer preferences and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
