<p><p>The EAS (Electronic Article Surveillance) antennas market is primarily divided into two types: RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) and traditional EAS systems. RFID systems utilize radio waves for tracking and managing inventory, enhancing security and efficiency in retail environments. Traditional EAS involves magnetic or electronic tags that trigger alarms when products are removed without proper deactivation. Both types serve the critical function of reducing theft and loss, but RFID offers additional benefits like real-time inventory management and data analytics capabilities.</p></p>
