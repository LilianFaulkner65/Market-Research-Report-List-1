The market for IRJs is segmented into two types: Supported IRJs, which are fixed to the rail ties, ensuring stability and alignment, and Suspended IRJs, which are designed to hang freely from the rail, accommodating track movements and reducing stress. Both types play vital roles in enhancing the safety and efficiency of rail operations.</p></p>
