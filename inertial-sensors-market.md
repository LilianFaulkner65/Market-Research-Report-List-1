<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inertial Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Attitude Heading and Reference Systems (AHRS)</li><li>Inertial Navigation Systems/Inertial Navigation Units (INUs)</li><li>Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inertial sensors market includes three main types: Attitude Heading and Reference Systems (AHRS), which provide orientation data for navigation; Inertial Navigation Systems/Inertial Navigation Units (INUs), which utilize measurements from accelerometers and gyroscopes to determine position and velocity over time; and Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs), which track motion and specific forces. Each type serves distinct applications in aerospace, automotive, and robotics, leveraging precision and reliability for navigation and motion tracking. Their growing demand reflects advancements in technology and increasing automation.</p></p>
