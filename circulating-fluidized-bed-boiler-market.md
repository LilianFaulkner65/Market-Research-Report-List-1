<p><p>The Circulating Fluidized Bed (CFB) Boiler market can be categorized into three types: subcritical, supercritical, and ultra-supercritical. </p><p>Subcritical CFB boilers operate below critical pressure and are typically used for smaller power plants, offering efficient fuel utilization. Supercritical CFB boilers function above the critical pressure, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions, making them suitable for medium to large-scale applications. Ultra-supercritical CFB boilers operate at even higher pressures and temperatures, achieving the highest efficiency and lowest emissions, ideal for large, modern power facilities.</p></p>
