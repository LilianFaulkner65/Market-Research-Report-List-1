<p><strong>The Linear Valve Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear valve actuators market primarily includes diaphragm and piston types. Diaphragm actuators utilize a flexible membrane to convert fluid pressure into linear motion, making them ideal for precise control in applications requiring low to moderate force. In contrast, piston actuators employ a cylindrical piston moved by hydraulic or pneumatic pressure, providing greater force and speed, suited for larger valves or more demanding operations. Both types play crucial roles in industrial automation and process control environments.</p></p>
