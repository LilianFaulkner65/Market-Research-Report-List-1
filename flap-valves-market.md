<p><p>Flap valves in the market are categorized into Type I and Type II based on their design and application. Type I flap valves are typically used in sewage and wastewater systems, designed for backflow prevention, offering a straightforward mechanism to allow flow in one direction while preventing reverse flow. Type II flap valves, on the other hand, are more versatile, utilized in various industrial applications, including stormwater management. They provide enhanced sealing capabilities, ensuring efficient operation under varying pressure conditions.</p></p>
