<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bio-based Aviation Fuel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HEFA-SPK</li><li>FT-SPK</li><li>ATJ-SPK</li><li>Co-processing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bio-based aviation fuel market includes several types: </p><p>1. **HEFA-SPK** (Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) is derived from oils and fats through hydroprocessing, leading to a drop-in fuel compatible with existing engines.</p><p>2. **FT-SPK** (Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) is produced from biomass using the Fischer-Tropsch process, creating a high-quality synthetic fuel.</p><p>3. **ATJ-SPK** (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) converts alcohols into jet fuel through catalytic processes.</p><p>4. **Co-processing** involves refining bio feedstocks alongside fossil fuels in existing refineries.</p><p>5. **Others** encompass various emerging technologies and feedstocks contributing to sustainable aviation fuel production, enhancing environmental sustainability.</p></p>
