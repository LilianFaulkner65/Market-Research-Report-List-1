<p><strong>The Ceramic Electrostatic Chuck for Semiconductor Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic electrostatic chucks (ESC) are critical in semiconductor equipment for wafer handling during processes like etching and deposition. The Coulomb type ESC utilizes electrostatic forces generated by surface charges to hold wafers firmly in place, offering precise control and uniform pressure. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type employs an electrodynamic principle, using a combination of electric fields and dielectric materials to achieve wafer clamping, renowned for its efficiency and reduced power consumption, catering to advanced semiconductor manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
