<p><ul><li>Thermopile Infrared Sensor</li><li>Pyroelectric Infrared Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared sensor market primarily features two types: thermopile and pyroelectric sensors. Thermopile infrared sensors detect temperature variations by converting infrared radiation into a measurable voltage, making them ideal for non-contact temperature measurement applications. In contrast, pyroelectric infrared sensors are designed to detect motion and changes in heat, commonly used in security systems and occupancy sensing. Both sensor types leverage infrared technology, catering to diverse applications spanning industrial, consumer electronics, and automotive sectors, enhancing efficiency and automation.</p></p>
