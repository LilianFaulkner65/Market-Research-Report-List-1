<p><p>The electron microscopy market comprises various types, including Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM), Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM), and others. TEM allows for high-resolution imaging by transmitting electrons through a specimen, revealing its internal structure. SEM, on the other hand, scans the surface of samples with focused electron beams to produce detailed, three-dimensional images. Other types may include specialized systems like Scanning Transmission Electron Microscopes or hybrid models, catering to specific research and industrial applications within materials science, biology, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
