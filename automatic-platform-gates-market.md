<p><p>The Automatic Platform Gates market comprises two primary types: Full Height Closed Automatic Platform Gates and Half Height Open Automatic Platform Gates. Full height gates enhance safety and security by preventing access to rail platforms while trains are approaching, making them ideal for high-speed or urban rail environments. Conversely, half height gates provide a balance between safety and visibility, allowing for easier passenger movement and interaction while still offering protection. Both types serve to improve safety and operational efficiency in transit systems.</p></p>
