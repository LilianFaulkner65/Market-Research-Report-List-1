<p><p>Metamaterials for antenna applications are categorized into several types. Negative-index metamaterials (NIM) exhibit both negative permittivity and permeability, enabling unique electromagnetic behavior. Single Negative (SNG) metamaterials possess either negative permittivity or permeability, enhancing specific antenna performance characteristics. Hyperbolic metamaterials (HMMs) allow for effective control of electromagnetic waves across a range of frequencies, improving bandwidth and resolution. Additionally, "Others" encompass various innovative metamaterial designs and configurations that contribute to advancements in antenna technology, enhancing efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
