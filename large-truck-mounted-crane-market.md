<p><p>The large truck-mounted crane market primarily features three types: folding boom, telescopic boom, and truss boom cranes. Folding boom cranes utilize a series of joints to efficiently extend and retract, enhancing mobility and compactness. Telescopic boom cranes consist of multiple sections that slide within each other, providing significant vertical reach and versatility. Truss boom cranes employ a framework design, offering improved strength-to-weight ratios, making them suitable for heavy lifting in challenging conditions. Each type caters to specific operational needs in construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
