<p><ul><li>Standard Translucency (ST)</li><li>High Translucency</li><li>Ultra High Translucency (UHT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zirconia Disc market comprises three primary types based on translucency levels: Standard Translucency (ST), High Translucency (HT), and Ultra High Translucency (UHT). ST discs offer basic aesthetics and strength for general dental applications. HT discs provide enhanced translucency, making them suitable for anterior restorations while maintaining durability. UHT discs deliver superior esthetics, closely mimicking natural teeth, ideal for high-demand cosmetic procedures. Each type caters to specific clinical needs, balancing aesthetics and functional performance in dental restorations.</p></p>
