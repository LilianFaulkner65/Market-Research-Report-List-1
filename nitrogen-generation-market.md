<p><ul><li>PSA Nitrogen Generators</li><li>Membrane Nitrogen Generators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrogen generation market consists primarily of two technologies: PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) nitrogen generators and membrane nitrogen generators. PSA generators utilize adsorption principles to separate nitrogen from air, offering high purity and pressure control, making them ideal for industries requiring large volumes of high-purity nitrogen. In contrast, membrane generators leverage selective permeability of membranes to separate nitrogen from other gases, providing a more compact and lower-cost solution for applications with moderate purity requirements. Together, they cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
