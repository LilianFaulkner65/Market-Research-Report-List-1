<p><p>The lumbar disc replacement devices market is categorized into metal-on-metal and metal-on-biopolymer types. Metal-on-metal devices feature both components made from durable metals, offering strong support and resistance to wear. These devices are known for their longevity but may carry risks of adverse reactions. In contrast, metal-on-biopolymer devices combine metal with biopolymer materials, aiming to provide a more favorable interface with surrounding tissues, potentially reducing complications and enhancing biological compatibility while maintaining mechanical stability.</p></p>
