<p><p>Infrared diffuse reflective photoelectric sensors are essential in detecting objects through emitted infrared light, which reflects off surfaces. The market is divided into two types based on output signals: digital and analog. Digital signal sensors provide a binary output, indicating object presence or absence, making them ideal for simple applications. In contrast, analog signal sensors offer continuous output proportional to the distance or reflectivity of an object, allowing for more nuanced measurements in complex automation and industrial settings.</p></p>
