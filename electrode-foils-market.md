<p><p>The electrode foils market comprises two primary segments: cathode foils and anode foils. Cathode foils are typically used in batteries and energy storage systems, facilitating efficient electron flow to enhance battery performance. Anode foils, on the other hand, play a crucial role in lithium-ion batteries, as they store lithium ions during discharge. Both types of foils are essential for optimizing energy efficiency, increasing battery lifespan, and supporting the growth of electric vehicles and renewable energy technologies.</p></p>
