<p><ul><li>Multiple Displacements</li><li>Single Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic piston pumps market is segmented into two main types: multiple displacement and single displacement pumps. Multiple displacement pumps can adjust their output flow by varying the number of active pistons, allowing for flexibility in applications and efficient fluid handling. In contrast, single displacement pumps have a fixed output, providing consistent flow rates but less adaptability. Both types serve distinct industrial needs, with multiple displacement pumps typically favored in dynamic systems requiring variable flow, while single displacement pumps are used for stable conditions.</p></p>
