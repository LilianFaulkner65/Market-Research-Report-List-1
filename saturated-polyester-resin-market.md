<p><p>The saturated polyester resin market comprises two main types: hybrid saturated polyester resins and pure saturated polyester resins. Hybrid resins combine features of both saturated polyesters and other resin types, offering enhanced performance characteristics like improved flexibility and weather resistance. In contrast, pure saturated polyester resins are derived solely from saturated polyols and acids, providing excellent durability, chemical resistance, and color stability. Both types are widely used in coatings, adhesives, and composites, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
