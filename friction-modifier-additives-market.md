<p><p>Friction modifier additives enhance the lubricating properties of fluids by reducing friction between surfaces. The market includes several types: </p><p>- **Polymers** provide viscosity stabilization and film strength. </p><p>- **Fatty acids** improve lubricity and reduce wear. </p><p>- **Esters and amides** offer excellent thermal stability and lubrication efficiency. </p><p>- **Graphite** acts as a solid lubricant, reducing wear in high-friction environments. </p><p>- **Molybdenum disulfide (MoS2)** forms a protective film, while </p><p>- **Molybdenum dithiocarbamate (MoDTC)** enhances anti-friction properties and thermal stability. Each type serves unique applications across various industries.</p></p>
